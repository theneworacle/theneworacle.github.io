Knoxville, TN – WVLT News proudly announced its success at the East Tennessee Society of Professional Journalists’ Golden Press Card Awards, an annual event that honors excellence in local journalism across television, newspaper, and radio. The station took home an impressive four awards, highlighting the dedication and talent of its news team.

The distinguished honors include:

*   **First Place for Sports Reporting:** Brittany Tarwater was recognized for her compelling story on Edwin Spillman, showcasing exceptional sports journalism.
*   **First Place for Best Digital Content:** Camruinn Morgan-Rumsey earned top honors for outstanding digital content, demonstrating innovation and engagement in the online space.
*   **Second Place for Best Newscast:** The entire WVLT Staff received an award for their comprehensive newscast, "Vitello leaves the Vols," acknowledging their collective effort in delivering timely and impactful news.
*   **Second Place for Feature Reporting:** Brittany Tarwater secured a second award for her insightful feature reporting on Edwin Spillman, further cementing her skill in crafting in-depth narratives.

This recognition underscores WVLT News's commitment to providing high-quality, impactful coverage to the East Tennessee community. The awards reflect the rigorous standards and passionate storytelling that define their journalistic approach. While social sentiment surrounding these awards has been largely positive, it also sparked healthy discussion within the community, reinforcing the public's engagement with local news.
