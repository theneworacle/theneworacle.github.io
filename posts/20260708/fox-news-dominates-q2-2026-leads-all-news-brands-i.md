Fox News Media closed out the second quarter of 2026 with an undeniable lead in digital viewership and engagement, significantly outpacing all other news brands on YouTube and across major social media platforms. The latest figures reveal a commanding performance, highlighting Fox News's continued dominance in the online news landscape.

According to data from Emplifi, Fox News garnered a staggering **1.4 billion YouTube video views** in Q2 2026. This figure not only placed them at the top but also more than doubled the viewership of CNN (511 million views) and significantly outranked MS NOW (763 million views). In a testament to its reach, Fox News's YouTube views surpassed the combined totals of ABC News (337 million), NBC News (310 million), and CBS News (136 million).

This marks the **seventh consecutive quarter** that Fox News has held the top spot among all news brands on YouTube, demonstrating a sustained and growing audience. Since the beginning of 2026, Fox News Media has collectively generated 3.3 billion YouTube video views, with the flagship Fox News Channel contributing a substantial 2.5 billion of those.

The success wasn't limited to YouTube. Fox News also led in overall social engagement during Q2 2026, racking up an impressive **269 million interactions** across Facebook, Instagram, X (formerly Twitter), and TikTok. Breaking down these numbers, the network saw 122 million interactions on Facebook, 51.9 million on Instagram, 18.2 million on X, and 76.8 million on TikTok (as reported by Comscore Social).

Beyond general news, Fox Business also showcased strong performance on YouTube, generating 158 million video views throughout the quarter. This achievement secured its position as the number one business news competitive set for the **56th consecutive month**, further solidifying Fox News Media's comprehensive digital leadership.

Social sentiment surrounding Fox News's Q2 2026 performance is largely positive, with ongoing discussions reflecting its prominent position in the media landscape.
