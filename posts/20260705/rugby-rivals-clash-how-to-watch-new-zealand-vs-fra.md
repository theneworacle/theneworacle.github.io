### The Nations Championship: A New Era for Rugby

This new biennial competition is designed to unify the international rugby calendar, bringing together the best teams from both hemispheres. The Northern contingent comprises the Six Nations sides, while the Southern Hemisphere is represented by the Rugby Championship nations, along with invitational teams Japan and Fiji. After six rounds of thrilling fixtures, a Finals Weekend will see teams compete based on their geographic group standings, culminating in a grand final between the top northern and southern sides. A parallel Ryder Cup-style 'Hemisphere Group Challenge' adds another layer of excitement.
